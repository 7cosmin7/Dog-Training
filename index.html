Who Am I

Hello and welcome to my website. Thank you for taking your time and stopping by! My name is Martina and I'm originally from the Czech Republic. I have always been driven towards nature and animals especialy. In the age of 13 I've been able to handle and train a dog that had no routine, boundaries and was full of energy. Ever since then I wanted to be a vet but I quickly realised that my real passion is to work with dogs and follow the path of dog behaviourist. I have been now studying and partly working in the field for past two years.

I have spent last few years studying, researching, gone through countless numbers of books and documentary movies and practise. Some of my studies includes knowledge in
Canine Psychology and Behaviour Management
and also
Practical Dog Behaviourist Studies
and practice on many different dogs. I found the practical side the most helpful because the theory is not always applyable and i learnt that every dog needs different approach. The best teacher is my dog that I practise on every day, he helps me to understand better their communication and thought me that patience, calmness and care are the best tools when dealing with a dog.
